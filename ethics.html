IT Ethics

Ethics in information technology focuses on how technology should be used responsibly. Since IT professionals often work with sensitive data and systems, it is important to follow ethical guidelines.

Privacy

Protecting user privacy is one of the most important responsibilities in IT. Personal information should only be collected when necessary and must be kept secure.

Security

IT systems should be designed to prevent unauthorized access and protect against cyberattacks. Strong security practices help keep user data safe.

Responsibility

IT professionals should be honest and accountable for their work. This includes reporting issues, avoiding misuse of data, and following company policies.
